Running Further Than You Thought Possible

Ultra running isn't just for elite athletes. It's for anyone willing to take the next step — whether that's your first trail 5K or your first 50-miler. This is your community.

Everything You Need to Get Started

Most people who want to run an ultra don't know where to begin. They're Googling gear they can't afford, training plans that don't fit their schedule, and races that feel out of reach. This site cuts through all of that. Real advice on gear, training, and finding your first race — written by someone who figured it out while working a full-time job and raising a family.

Gear

You don't need the latest carbon-plated everything. You need shoes that fit, a vest that doesn't bounce, and food your stomach won't revolt against at mile 30. We cover what actually matters on the trail and how to get it without spending a fortune. Last year's model runs just as far as this year's — and costs a lot less.

Training

The best training plan is the one you can actually stick to. If you have a job, a family, and about an hour on a good day, this is built for you. We cover how to build mileage without breaking down, how to adjust when life gets in the way, and why consistency beats perfection every single time.

Races

Picking your first ultra doesn't have to be overwhelming. The right race isn't the most famous one or the hardest one — it's the one that gives you the best shot at crossing the finish line. We break down what to look for, share beginner-friendly races worth knowing, and tell you exactly what to expect on race day.
